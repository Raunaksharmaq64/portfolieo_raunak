### 🏫 HostelBuddy — Find Your Perfect Stay
> A comprehensive full-stack platform designed to connect students and working professionals with verified hostels and PG accommodations without middlemen.

* **Tech Stack:** `Node.js`, `Express.js`, `MongoDB`, `HTML5`, `CSS3`, `JavaScript (ES6+)`
* **Key Features:**
  * Smart discovery filters based on budget, gender, location, and amenities.
  * Direct contact system enabling users to send enquiries straight to property owners.
  * Image galleries showcasing rooms, common areas, and amenities.
  * User rating & review system for genuine feedback.
  * Admin dashboard for property verification and listing management.
  * Accompanied by an Android APK for mobile accessibility.
* **Links:**
